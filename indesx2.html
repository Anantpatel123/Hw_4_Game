Crystal Game

Game Rules!!!!

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your toal score.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

5. Each time when the game starts, the random number and the value of each crystal will change.
